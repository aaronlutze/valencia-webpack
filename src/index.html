VALENCIA

We are a local clothing label that specialises in producing nothing at all, including jackets that will never me made, shoes that will never be run in, and hats that will never protect from any sun.

We do this because we are young, and we will never afford the Australian dream, so we've chosen to dedicate our lives to planned obsolescence. This is all we can do, there really isn't anything more.
